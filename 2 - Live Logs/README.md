# Live Logs

> The raw signal of your life. Append-only.

Files to create here:
- `ACTIONS_LOG.md` — things you did
- `DECISIONS_LOG.md` — choices you made and why
- `EMOTIONS_LOG.md` — how you felt and what triggered it
- `LEARNING_LOG.md` — things you learned
- `REFLECTIONS_LOG.md` — patterns you noticed
- `ASSISTANT_ACTIONS_LOG.md` — things Claude did on your behalf

All logs are append-only. Never rewrite history.
