# Attribution

The Evolving Brain borrows ideas, patterns, and sometimes prompt text from several prior-art projects.

## Sources

### garrytan/gbrain
**Repo:** https://github.com/garrytan/gbrain  
**License:** MIT

**What we borrowed:**
- The "compiled truth on top, append-only timeline on bottom" entity page pattern
- The "dream cycle" nightly maintenance concept
- The recommended entity schema: persistent IDs, source attribution, citations
- The `query`, `maintain`, `briefing`, `migrate` workflow patterns

### farzaa's wiki-gen-skill
**Source:** https://gist.github.com/farzaa/c35ac0cfbeb957788650e36aabea836d

**What we borrowed:**
- The philosophy: "You are a writer compiling, not a filing clerk."
- The Wikipedia-style visual target
- The organic directory emergence idea

### Tiago Forte's PARA method
**Source:** Tiago Forte, "Building a Second Brain"

**What we borrowed:**
- The PARA naming and structure: Projects, Areas, Resources, Archive
- The principle that everything is either actionable or reference material
- The archive-over-delete convention
